# Page Brief: RingAnalytics

## Page Purpose
Convert practice owners who are spending money on marketing but have no idea which channels are actually producing booked appointments, and who have no visibility into why callers don't convert — by showing them that RingAnalytics turns the phone from a black box into a measurable revenue engine.

## Target Audience
1. **Primary:** Practice owners/ODs who spend on marketing but can't prove ROI
2. **Secondary:** Office managers who manage front desk staff and need coaching tools
3. **Tertiary:** Multi-location group owners who need to compare performance across practices

### 7. Objections / FAQ (4-5 items)
1. "Big Brother" — staff see recording as micromanagement → frame as protection, not punishment
2. HIPAA & Consent — legal concerns → BAA, encryption, auto-redaction, 50-state consent greeting
3. "Analysis Paralysis" — too many dashboards → exception-based reporting, 5-minute weekly review
4. "Impersonal Patient" — recording message scares patients → transcript enables continuity of care
5. "I already have CallRail/Weave" → tracking calls ≠ tracking revenue

### 8. Comparison Table
RingAnalytics vs. Patient Prism vs. CallRail vs. Weave vs. Call Box
Key differentiators: optometry-trained AI, booking attribution (not just call attribution), HIPAA included, practice-level pricing
