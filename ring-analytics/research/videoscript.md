# Video Script: RingAnalytics (2-minute overview)

## Opening Hook (0:00 - 0:15)
"How many patients called your practice last month? Now — how many of those calls actually resulted in a booked appointment? If you don't know the answer, you're not alone. Most optometry practices have zero visibility into what happens on the phone."

## The Problem (0:15 - 0:35)
"85% of patients still book over the phone. But the phone is a black box. You spend thousands on marketing — Google Ads, your website, your Google Business Profile — and patients call. But did they book? If they didn't, why not? Was it an insurance issue? Were they put on hold too long? Did your staff forget to ask for the appointment? Without data, you're guessing."

## Introducing RingAnalytics (0:35 - 0:55)
"RingAnalytics changes that. Every call to your practice is automatically recorded, transcribed, and scored by AI. Each call is tagged: booked or not booked, new patient or returning, and which marketing channel drove it — Google, your website, or an online directory."

## How It Works (0:55 - 1:20)
"Setup is transparent. Calls route through RingAnalytics before reaching your phones — your patients and staff notice nothing different. The AI analyzes every call and you get a dashboard showing exactly what happened. See your top-performing marketing channels. See which calls converted and which didn't. Click any call to read the transcript or listen to the recording."

## The Revenue Impact (1:20 - 1:40)
"In Q1 2026, RingAnalytics tracked 324,000 calls across 189 practices. 70% of appointment calls converted to bookings. That means 30% didn't — roughly 25,000 potential patients who called but didn't book. At $350 per exam, that's nearly $9 million in potential lost revenue. Even recovering 2-3 lost bookings a month pays for the entire platform."

## Close (1:40 - 2:00)
"RingAnalytics is built specifically for optometry. Our AI understands your calls — exam inquiries, insurance questions, optical orders, contact lens follow-ups. It's HIPAA compliant out of the box. And it integrates with the PatientEngage platform you already use. Stop guessing. Start measuring. See your first call report within 24 hours."
