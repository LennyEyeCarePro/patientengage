# Benefits: RingAnalytics

## Primary Benefits

### 1. Marketing Attribution — Know What's Actually Ringing Your Phone
Every call is tagged with Source and Medium (GMB, Google Ads, website, offsite directories). Practice owners can see exactly which marketing channels produce booked appointments — not just clicks, not just calls, but actual patients in chairs. Stop guessing which ads work. Stop paying for "dead" leads.

### 2. Conversion Visibility — See the "Leaky Bucket"
20-30% of new patient inquiries fail to book. Without scoring, these lost opportunities vanish. An owner might see high call volume and think the practice is busy while actually losing thousands in potential revenue every week. RingAnalytics scores every call as Booked/Not Booked and tags New vs. Returning patients, creating a "Lost Opportunity" report that turns invisible losses into recoverable revenue.

### 3. Staff Training with Data, Not Opinions
Practice owners cannot sit at the front desk all day. Staff may be "nice" but not "effective." Transcription and call scoring provide objective data for coaching. Instead of "be better on the phone," an owner can say: "On Tuesday, we had 5 new patient calls over 4 minutes that didn't book. Let's look at the transcripts together."

### 4. Multi-Practice Comparison
For groups and multi-location owners: compare performance across all practices instantly. See which locations have the highest capture rates, which are missing the most calls, and which need staff training. The admin dashboard shows all 189+ practices side by side with sortable columns.

### 5. Call-Back Revenue Recovery
When a "New Patient" call from GMB is marked "Not Booked," the practice can review the transcript to see WHY (insurance issue? rude greeting? no available slots?) and call that patient back within the hour to save a $400+ lead.

### 6. Peak Staffing Optimization
Track call volume throughout the day to find optimal staffing times. Know when you're missing calls. Know when phones are busiest. Staff the phones based on data, not guesswork.

## The Revenue Math
- Average exam value: $350
- 30% of appointment calls don't convert = ~25,000 lost opportunities per quarter across the network
- If a practice recovers even 5 of those lost calls per month = $1,750/month in recovered revenue
- RingAnalytics pays for itself by recovering 1-2 missed bookings per month
