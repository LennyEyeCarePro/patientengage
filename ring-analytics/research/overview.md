# Overview: RingAnalytics

## What It Is
RingAnalytics is an AI-powered call tracking, recording, transcription, and scoring platform that transforms the phone — traditionally a "black box" of lost data — into a measurable revenue engine for optometry practices.

## The Problem It Solves
85% of patients still book appointments over the phone (Healthgrades/Stax). But most practice owners have zero visibility into what happens on those calls. They don't know how many calls convert to bookings, which marketing channels are driving the phone to ring, or why patients who call don't end up on the schedule. The phone is the single most important patient touchpoint, and it's completely unmeasured.

## How It Works
Every inbound call to the practice is routed through the HIPAA-compliant RingAnalytics platform. Each call is:

1. **Recorded** — Full audio capture with HIPAA-compliant storage and encryption
2. **Transcribed** — AI generates a complete text transcript of every call
3. **Scored** — AI classifies each call as Booked/Not Booked, New Patient/Returning Patient
4. **Attributed** — Source and Medium tracking (GMB, Google Ads, website, offsite directories) shows which marketing channels are driving calls
5. **Analyzed** — Call duration, keywords, and patterns are tracked to identify training opportunities

### Per-Call Data Captured:
- Caller phone number
- Caller name (when available)
- Office number / location
- Source (OnSite, OffSite)
- Medium (GMB, Google Ads, Other Directories)
- Call duration
- Call type (booked / not booked)
- Patient type (new / returning)
- Date and time
- Keywords tagged
- Full transcript
- Full audio recording

## Key Numbers (Q1 2026: Jan 1 - Mar 30, Network-Wide)
- **189 practices**, 484 locations
- **324,348 total calls** tracked (↑50% YoY)
- **83,717 appointment calls** (26% of all calls) (↑68%)
- **58,418 booked calls** (70% of appointment calls) (↑71%)
- **Average call length:** 2:48
- **3,810 new patients** identified
- **9,251 returning patients** identified

### Alerts (Practices Flagged):
- Call Drop >25%: 14 practices (↓22% — improving)
- Appt Calls <35%: 235 practices (flagged for low appointment call ratio)
- Appt Capture <60%: 75 practices (flagged for low conversion)
- Missed Calls >15%: 207 practices (flagged for high missed call rate)

## Why It Matters
If 26% of calls are appointment-related and 70% of those convert, that means 30% of appointment calls — roughly 25,000 potential bookings in Q1 — did NOT convert. At $350 per comprehensive exam, that represents $8.75 million in potential lost revenue across the network in a single quarter. RingAnalytics makes this invisible problem visible and actionable.

## Built for Optometry
Unlike generic call tracking tools (CallRail, CallTrackingMetrics), RingAnalytics understands optometry-specific call patterns: new patient intake, exam type inquiries, insurance questions, optical order status, and contact lens follow-ups. The AI scoring is trained on optometry call data from 189 practices — not generic healthcare or dental conversations.
