# Case Study: 2-Way Texting — Lakewood Eye Center

## Practice Profile
- **Anonymized Name:** Lakewood Eye Center
- **Real Practice:** Vista Eye Center
- **Location:** Huffman, TX (single location)
- **Address:** 11747 FM 1960 Suite A-102, Huffman, TX 77336
- **Practice Type:** Full-service optometry
- **Website:** WordPress (v3) with PatientEngage integration
- **Data Period:** Q1 2026 (January 1 – March 30)

## The Challenge
Lakewood Eye Center is a single-location optometry practice in a suburban Houston community. Like most practices, they were relying almost entirely on phone calls for patient communication — leading to missed calls, phone tag, and patients who gave up before scheduling. The practice needed a faster, more convenient way to connect with patients without adding staff or complexity.

## The Numbers (Q1 2026)

### Text Volume
- **Total Texts:** 1,805 (highest in the 278-practice network)
- **Average per month:** ~602 texts/month
- **Average per business day:** ~28 texts/day

### Read Performance
- **Read:** 1,793 (99% read rate)
- **Unread:** 2 (<1%)
- **Read Rate:** 99% — highest-volume practice in the network with near-perfect read engagement

### Context: Network Position
- **#1 in total text volume** out of 278 practices (455 locations)

- **Only 2 unread messages** in an entire quarter — indicating the practice checks and responds to texts consistently

## Revenue Math

### Call Deflection Value
- With ~28 texts/day handling patient questions, appointment requests, and follow-ups, the practice deflects a significant portion of inbound calls
- Average inbound call handling cost: ~$5–$7 per call (staff time, hold time, missed opportunity cost)
- If even 50% of texts replace calls that would have been made: ~14 calls deflected/day × $6 avg cost = **$84/day in operational savings**
- Quarterly savings estimate: **$5,460**

### Conversion Value
- Texts capture patients at the moment of highest intent (visiting the website or texting directly)
- If even 10% of text conversations (180 per quarter) result in a booked appointment: 180 × $350 avg exam value = **$63,000 in text-influenced revenue per quarter**
- Annual projection: **$252,000** in text-influenced revenue

### Missed Opportunity Prevention
- Every text that gets a response is a patient who didn't go to voicemail, didn't hang up, and didn't call the practice down the street
- With a 99% read rate, virtually no patient message goes unanswered

## What 2-Way Texting Revealed

1. **1,805 texts in a single quarter from one location** — this volume proves patients prefer texting over calling when given the option. The practice gave patients a "Text Us" button on their website and patients used it heavily.

2. **99% read rate means the practice is engaged** — the tool only works if staff actually check it. Lakewood Eye Center made text monitoring part of their daily workflow, and the result is near-zero unread messages.

3. **28 texts per day replaces significant call volume** — each text conversation that answers a patient's question about hours, insurance, or appointment availability is a call the front desk didn't have to handle.

4. **Highest volume in a 278-practice network** — a single-location practice outpaced multi-location groups, proving that adoption and workflow integration matter more than practice size.
