# Step 6: Pre-Gen Q&A — 2-Way Text Messaging

## Confirmed Answers

### 1. What is the primary angle for this page?
**Answer:** "Every missed call is a missed patient — give them a faster way to reach you." Position texting as the modern, simple, human-first communication channel that competes on **speed, price, and simplicity** — NOT features. Lead with the problem (phone tag, missed calls, overwhelmed front desk, automation fatigue) and solve it with included, human-first texting.

### 2. What is the killer stat?
**Answer:** 88% read rate across 278 practices (14,327 texts in Q1 2026). For case study: 1,805 texts from a single location with 99% read rate. The "5-10 text conversations in the time of one phone call" operational stat. The $3,000–$6,000/year "bundle tax" that competitors charge.

### 3. Who is the target persona?
**Answer:** Primary: Office manager / front desk lead drowning in calls and voicemail loops (15–20 hours/month in phone tag). Secondary: Practice owner (OD) tired of paying $400–$700/month for bundled suites they only use 20% of. Tertiary: Practices already using EyeCarePro who don't realize texting is included.

### 4. What is the feature name as patients/practices would say it?
**Answer:** "Text messaging" or "texting" or "the Text Us button." Practices call it "2-way texting." Patients see "Text Us" on the website. Position it as a "Digital Front Door" — not another "app."

### 5. What is the primary CTA?
**Answer:** "Start Texting Your Patients Today" — with secondary: "Already an EyeCarePro client? You may already have this included." The "one-login advantage" for existing clients.

### 6. What are the true differentiators vs. competitors?
**Answer:**
1. **Included in marketing programs** — no "bundle tax" ($3,000–$6,000/year avoided)
2. **Human-first, no bots** — the "High-Touch Channel" vs. "Remind-O-Bots"
3. **Zero-friction deployment** — live in 15 minutes vs. weeks of EHR mapping
4. **EHR independence** — works regardless of software, doesn't break when EHR updates
5. **One-login advantage** — same dashboard as RingAnalytics, SEO, marketing
6. **Read rate tracking** — know if the patient actually read your message

### 7. Who are the real competitors?
**Answer (ranked by direct threat):**
1. **OhMD** — Most direct. Simple standalone HIPAA texting. Free tier. Counter: we're included in marketing, one-login advantage.
2. **Podium** — Website lead capture widget king. $400–$600/month. Counter: price and focus — "Quiet Clinical Alternative" vs. "SaaS bloat."
3. **Rhinogram** — Specialty healthcare (optometry, ortho). Photo/file sharing. Counter: EHR independence is a feature, not a bug.
4. **Weave/Solutionreach/RevenueWell** — The "Bundlers." Automated, expensive, EHR-dependent. Counter: automation fatigue, integration friction, bundle tax.

### 8. What are the top 3 benefits (ROI + operational)?
**Answer:**
1. **High-Velocity Lead Capture ("Anti-Bounce")** — Capture website visitors at peak intent before they bounce to competitors. Text conversation → booked exam.
2. **Elimination of Phone Tag (Operational Efficiency)** — Staff manages 5–10 texts in time of one call. Clears phone lines. Saves 15–20 hours/month of manual dialing.
3. **Frictionless Patient Experience (Brand Loyalty)** — 20–30% of lapse-prone patients view phone calls as a chore. Texting makes the practice feel modern and "easy to do business with."

### 9. What objections will the FAQ need to handle?
**Answer (7 key objections):**
1. "I already have Weave/Solutionreach" → They're "Remind-O-Bots." We're the Express Lane for real conversations.
2. "My staff is already buried" → Phone call = 5 min. Text = 30 sec. Texting clears phone lines.
3. "No EHR sync = double work" → For service questions, EHR sync is a liability. We're a clean "hotline" that works when your EHR is down.
4. "My patients are 70+ and don't text" → 75%+ seniors prefer text. Moving quick questions to text frees staff for the high-touch calls seniors want.
5. "After-hours boundaries" → Instant Auto-Reply captures the lead, protects the team.
6. "HIPAA anxiety" → The real risk is staff using personal phones. We're the "Secure Vault."
7. "Login fatigue" → Not another app — it's a "Digital Front Door" that consolidates scattered leads.

The "Fast Track" that works regardless of what software you use. Position "Not connected to EHR" as a **feature** — zero setup time, no sync-lag, no broken integrations.
