# Product Knowledgebase: 2-Way Text Messaging

## Source
- EyeCarePro product positioning (internal brief)
- PatientEngage Admin: app.patientengage.ai/v2/a/reports/texts

- Note: No dedicated product page exists on EyeCarePro.com for text messaging

## Product Positioning
**Product Name:** 2-Way Texting
**Category:** Communications (alongside Forms, Calls, Announcements in PE nav)
**Tagline:** Simple, Fast Patient Communication
**Core Purpose:** Make it easier for patients to reach you and get answers fast.

### Key Messaging
- A quick way for patients to text your practice directly from your website or their phone
- Your team replies through a secure, HIPAA-safe dashboard
- Included in all marketing programs or available as a standalone service
- Not automated — this is real human-to-human conversation
- Not connected to the EHR
- Not a replacement for a full engagement platform — it's a communication channel

## How It Works
1. **Patient-Initiated:** Patient sends a text via "Text Us" button on the practice website or directly to the practice's texting number
2. **Practice-Initiated:** Practice can start a text thread at any time from the PE dashboard
3. **Real-Time Response:** Team receives and responds in real time through the secure dashboard
4. **HIPAA-Compliant:** All conversations are fully secure and encrypted
5. **Two-Way:** Both directions — practice can text patient, patient can text practice

### Important Distinctions
- **Not automated** — no chatbots, no auto-replies, no AI responses
- **Not connected to EHR** — standalone communication tool
- **Not a replacement** for a full engagement platform — pair with Recalls & Reminders or Real Time Scheduler for automation

## Feature List
1. Two-way text messaging between practice and patients
2. "Text Us" website button for patient-initiated conversations
3. Practice-initiated outbound texting from the dashboard
4. HIPAA-compliant secure messaging
5. Real-time conversation threading
6. Read/unread tracking per message
7. Per-location text management (multi-location support)
8. Admin reporting across all practices in the network

## Admin Platform Deep-Dive

### Network Overview (Q1 2026: Jan 1 – Mar 30)
- 278 practices, 455 locations using Text Messages
- 14,327 total texts sent across the network
- 12,592 read (88% read rate)
- 170 practices with ≥75% read rate
- 719 unread (5% of total)
- 225 practices flagged with ≥25% unread rate (alert threshold)

### Admin Report Columns
The admin Texts report shows per-location data across:
- Practice Name, City
- Read (count and % of total)
- Unread (count and % of total)
- Total (with link to detail view)

### Alert Thresholds
- ≥25% Unread: 225 practices flagged — indicates practices that may not be checking their text inbox regularly

### Filters Available
- **All Groups:** Filter by practice group
- **All CSs / MMs:** Filter by Customer Success / Marketing Manager (team member assigned to the account)
- **Date Range:** Custom date picker
- **Search:** Full-text search across practices
- **CSV Export:** Download button for all text data

### Practice Dashboard View
The practice-level text messaging interface (at `/u/{practiceId}/text-messages`) provides:
1. **Location Selector:** Multi-location practices choose which location's texts to view
2. **Conversation Thread View:** Individual text conversations with patients
3. **Send Button:** Initiate new text messages to patients
4. **"Text Us" Button:** Embeddable website widget for patient-initiated texts

## What Makes 2-Way Texting Different

1. **Human-first design:** No chatbots or auto-replies — every response is from a real staff member, building genuine patient relationships
2. **Optometry-specific deployment:** Deployed across 278 optometry practices with 455 locations — purpose-built for eyecare workflows
3. **Website integration:** "Text Us" button captures website visitors at the moment of highest intent
4. **HIPAA-compliant:** Full encryption and secure messaging — not consumer SMS apps
5. **Read tracking:** Know which messages patients actually opened — visibility into engagement that phone calls and voicemails can't provide
6. **Network-wide reporting:** Admin view shows text performance across all practices with alert thresholds
7. **Included in marketing programs:** Not an add-on cost — included in EyeCarePro marketing packages or available standalone
8. **Complements the stack:** Works alongside RingAnalytics (calls), Scheduler (online booking), and Recalls & Reminders (automated outreach) as part of the PatientEngage communications suite

## Best Use Case
Use 2-way texting when you want simple, real-time communication that helps your staff stay connected to patients. For automation or scheduling tools, pair it with services like Recalls & Reminders or the Real Time Scheduler.
