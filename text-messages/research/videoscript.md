# Video Script: 2-Way Text Messaging

## Format
- **Length:** 60–90 seconds
- **Style:** Screen recording walkthrough with voiceover
- **Audience:** Practice owners and office managers considering PatientEngage

---

## Script

### Opening (0:00–0:10)
**[Show: Practice phone ringing, going to voicemail]**

**Voiceover:** "Your patients are calling — but are they getting through? Every missed call, every voicemail, every round of phone tag is a patient who might not come back."

### Problem (0:10–0:20)
**[Show: Stats — 85% book by phone, but missed calls are rising]**

**Voiceover:** "Most patients still pick up the phone to book an appointment. But when they can't get through, they don't leave a message — they search for someone else."

### Solution Intro (0:20–0:35)
**[Show: "Text Us" button appearing on a practice website]**

**Voiceover:** "2-Way Texting gives your patients a faster way to reach you. A simple 'Text Us' button on your website lets patients start a conversation instantly — no hold time, no phone tag."

### How It Works (0:35–0:55)
**[Show: PE dashboard — text conversation thread, staff typing a reply]**

**Voiceover:** "When a patient texts, your team sees the message in the secure PatientEngage dashboard. They reply in real time — answering questions, confirming appointments, helping patients get the care they need."

**[Show: Staff handling multiple conversations]**

**Voiceover:** "Your team can manage multiple text conversations at once — something that's impossible with phone calls."

### Proof (0:55–1:10)
**[Show: Admin texts report — network stats, read rates]**

**Voiceover:** "Across 278 practices, over 14,000 texts were sent last quarter with an 88% read rate. One practice alone handled 1,800 texts — that's 28 per day from a single location."

### Differentiator (1:10–1:20)
**[Show: Side-by-side — chatbot response vs. real staff response]**

**Voiceover:** "And unlike chatbots and automated platforms, every response comes from a real person on your team. Your patients get genuine answers — not canned replies."

### Close (1:20–1:30)
**[Show: PatientEngage logo + CTA]**

**Voiceover:** "2-Way Texting is included in all EyeCarePro marketing programs. No extra cost. No complex setup. Just a better way to connect with your patients. Start texting today."
