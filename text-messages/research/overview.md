# Overview: 2-Way Text Messaging

## What It Is
2-Way Texting is a HIPAA-compliant patient messaging tool that lets optometry practices send and receive text messages with patients directly from the PatientEngage dashboard. Patients can also initiate conversations by tapping a "Text Us" button on the practice's website.

## The Problem It Solves
Patients call the office, get put on hold, leave a voicemail, and never hear back. Or they hang up and search for another practice. Meanwhile, the front desk is overwhelmed with call volume and can't get to everyone. The phone — still the primary patient touchpoint — creates friction on both sides. Patients want quick answers. Staff want fewer interruptions. But there's no fast, simple way to connect that doesn't involve picking up the phone.

## How It Works
1. **Patient texts the practice** — via a "Text Us" button on the website, or by texting the practice number directly
2. **Practice texts the patient** — staff can initiate a new text thread from the dashboard at any time
3. **Real-time conversation** — messages appear instantly in the secure dashboard; staff reply in real time
4. **HIPAA-compliant** — all messages are encrypted and stored securely
5. **Read tracking** — the admin dashboard tracks which messages have been read and which are still unread

### Important Distinctions
- **Not automated** — no chatbots, no auto-replies. Every response is from a real staff member.
- **Not connected to the EHR** — standalone communication tool, no PMS integration required.
- **Not a replacement for a full engagement platform** — it's a communication channel. Pair with Recalls & Reminders or the Real Time Scheduler for automation.

## Key Numbers (Q1 2026: Jan 1 – Mar 30, Network-Wide)
- **278 practices**, 455 locations using Text Messages
- **14,327 total texts** sent across the network
- **12,592 read** (88% read rate)
- **170 practices** with ≥75% read rate
- **719 unread** (5% of total)
- **225 practices** flagged with ≥25% unread rate

- Offices offering specialty services that generate more questions
- Practices struggling with phone tag and slow response times
- Teams that want a faster way to handle patient inquiries
