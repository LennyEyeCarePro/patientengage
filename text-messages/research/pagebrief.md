# Page Brief: 2-Way Text Messaging Marketing Page

## Page Goal
Convince optometry practice owners and office managers that 2-Way Texting solves their patient communication problems — reduce missed calls, capture more appointments, and give patients the modern experience they expect — all without adding another expensive platform.

## Target Audience
- Practice owners (ODs) who know their phones are overwhelmed
- Office managers who deal with phone tag, missed calls, and voicemail backlogs daily
- Multi-location groups looking for a communication channel that scales
- Practices already paying for EyeCarePro marketing who don't realize they have texting included

## Key Messages (Priority Order)
1. **Patients prefer texting** — 98% open rate, instant, no hold time
2. **Your front desk is drowning in calls** — texting deflects routine questions
3. **It's already included** — no $300–$500/month add-on like competitors
4. **Real humans, not bots** — every reply builds trust
5. **HIPAA-compliant** — not staff personal phones
6. **We can prove it works** — 14,327 texts, 88% read rate, real practice data
