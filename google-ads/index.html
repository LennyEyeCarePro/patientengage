The problem

Traditional Google Ads is running blind.
Your schedule is not.

Every practice has a natural fill rate. A percentage of your schedule that fills on its own, without any advertising. EHR-Integrated Google Ads calculates that number and spends your budget only on the gap.

The waste problem
It spends when you are already full.
Traditional Google Ads has no connection to your appointment schedule. It spends your budget every day whether you have open slots or not. If your next available appointment is two weeks out, patients click, find nothing, and leave. You paid for every one of those clicks.
The visibility problem
You have no idea if it is working.
Clicks and impressions are not patients. Your agency sends a monthly report full of metrics that cannot tell you how many people who saw your ad actually booked an appointment. You are guessing whether your $2,000 a month is doing anything.
The natural fill rate problem
You are paying for patients you were already going to get.
If your practice naturally fills to 70% every two-week period, the first 70% of your appointments do not need ads to fill them. Spending budget on those patients is pure waste. EHR-Integrated Google Ads identifies only the incremental gap and spends there.
The inventory problem
An unfilled slot is gone forever.
An open appointment at 2pm on Tuesday does not roll over to Wednesday. Every day your schedule runs below its historic average fill rate, that revenue is gone. Your schedule is perishable inventory and most Google Ads have no awareness of it whatsoever.
How it works

Three inputs. One system.
No wasted spend.

EHR-Integrated Google Ads connects directly to your EHR and processes three data points to make every spend decision.

1
Current Booking Rate
The system reads your EHR and sees exactly how many appointment slots are open for the next two weeks. It knows which days have capacity, which appointment types have gaps, and where the soft spots are.
2
Natural Fill Rate
Using historical EHR data and patient booking behavior, the system predicts how many of those open slots will fill on their own without any advertising. This is your baseline. Spend targets only what is above it.
3
Your CPA Target
You tell the system the maximum you are willing to pay per new patient. The system calculates exactly how much of your schedule it can fill at that price and shows you the projection before any spend is committed.

Common questions

Things practice owners ask
before saying yes.

"I can't afford to waste money on ads that don't work." ↓
The waste in traditional Google Ads is structural. The system spends your budget every day whether you need patients or not. EHR-Integrated Google Ads only spends when your schedule has open slots that will not fill on their own. Before any spend is committed, the simulation shows exactly what projected results look like at your budget level. You see the math before you spend a dollar.
"How do I know I'm paying for actual booked appointments and not just clicks?" ↓
EHR-Integrated Google Ads tracks two sources: appointments booked through the PatientEngage Scheduler on the landing page, and phone calls scored as booked appointments by Ring Analytics. Every conversion in your dashboard is a confirmed booked appointment. Standard Google Ads, and every other PPC agency, tracks lead actions: clicks, form fills, calls over 60 seconds. These are different metrics and should not be directly compared. A $6 cost per lead action and a $26 cost per confirmed booked appointment are not the same number.
"PPC just sends me price shoppers. I want quality medical patients." ↓
Generic PPC sends generic traffic. EHR-Integrated Google Ads uses your EHR data to optimize for the specific appointment types that generate the most revenue for your practice. The system learns which searches result in booked high-value appointments and allocates more budget to those. Large optical retailers compete on broad terms: eyeglasses, contacts, cheap eye exams. EHR-Integrated Google Ads targets the medical specialties where independent ODs have a genuine clinical advantage: dry eye treatment, myopia management, scleral lenses, comprehensive exams. Over time the quality of traffic improves as the model learns your practice.
"There are too many big retailers in my area. I can't compete with their budgets." ↓
Large optical retailers bid on broad terms. They do not optimize for dry eye clinics, scleral lens specialists, or myopia management programs. EHR-Integrated Google Ads targets the medical specialties and specific services where independent ODs have a genuine clinical advantage. These are searches the big retailers are not competing for. That is where the opportunity gap is, and most of your competitors are leaving it wide open.
"I don't want an algorithm spending my money. I want a human watching my ads." ↓
You set the strategy. You set the maximum CPA. You set the target booking rate. The algorithm executes within those parameters, adjusting bids in real time based on schedule availability, search intent, and historical performance. A human cannot adjust a bid at 3am based on a patient's search behavior. The algorithm can. The result is more efficient spend at every hour of the day, not just when someone is looking at a dashboard.

"I'm already running Google Ads with another agency. Why would I switch?" ↓

"I'm not comfortable connecting my EHR to a marketing platform. What about patient data security?" ↓
The EHR connection is read-only. The system reads appointment availability and booking patterns. It does not write to your EHR. It does not access patient clinical records.
