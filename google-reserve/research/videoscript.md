# Video Script: Google Reserve (90 seconds)

## Opening (0:00 - 0:12)
**Voiceover:** "Every day, patients search for your practice on Google. They see your listing, your reviews, your hours. And then... they have to leave Google, navigate your website, find your scheduler, and hope they don't give up."
**Screen:** Google Search results showing an optometry practice listing WITHOUT a Book online button. Patient cursor clicking "Website," then navigating through a multi-page practice site.

## The Problem (0:12 - 0:22)
**Voiceover:** "Every extra click is a patient you lose. You've already won their attention. Now you're losing their booking."
**Screen:** Animation showing a funnel: 100 Google views → 40 website clicks → 15 find scheduler → 8 book. Each step leaks patients.

## The Solution (0:22 - 0:38)
**Voiceover:** "Google Reserve puts a 'Book online' button directly on your Google Business Profile, in Search and Maps. Patients tap once, land on your booking page, and schedule. No website detour. No extra clicks."
**Screen:** Google Maps showing the practice listing with the prominent blue "Book online" banner. Patient taps it. Booking page loads instantly. Appointment confirmed.

## How It Works (0:38 - 0:52)
**Voiceover:** "Setting it up takes less time than making coffee. Open your PatientEngage dashboard, toggle Google Reserve on, choose where the button links, and you're live within 24 hours."
**Screen:** PatientEngage dashboard → Google Reserve settings → Toggle flipping to ON → Booking Type dropdown showing options (PatientEngage Scheduler, 3rd Party Link) → Green checkmark "Last Synced"

## The Flexibility (0:52 - 1:02)
**Voiceover:** "And here's what makes PatientEngage different: you choose where that button goes. Our scheduler, your website, any booking page you want. Your practice, your rules. You own the patient relationship."
**Screen:** Three destination options appearing: PE Scheduler page, practice website contact page, third-party scheduler. Each with a checkmark.

## The Results (1:02 - 1:18)
**Voiceover:** "A 3-location practice saw their Google Reserve clicks exceed their total website clicks in the first month. Their top location hit a 73% action rate. Over 227,000 patients clicked 'Book online' across our network last month alone."
**Screen:** Dashboard showing clicks chart trending up. Stats animating in: 696 clicks, 73% action rate, 227K+ network clicks. Map showing 1,200+ active locations lighting up.

## Close (1:18 - 1:30)
**Voiceover:** "Your patients are already searching. Give them the one button that turns a search into a booked appointment. Activate Google Reserve in your PatientEngage dashboard today."
**Screen:** Google Search showing practice with "Book online" button glowing/highlighted. Fade to PatientEngage logo. CTA: "Activate Google Reserve" button. URL: patientengage.com/google-reserve

## Production Notes
- Tone: Confident, direct, not overly salesy. Speak to the practice owner as a peer.
- Music: Light, modern background. Not corporate. Not urgent.
- Pacing: Brisk but not rushed. Let the data moments breathe.
- Graphics: Clean, minimal. Match Style B design system (navy, teal accents).
- No em dashes in any on-screen text.
- Anonymize the case study practice (do not name Simmons Eye Care).
