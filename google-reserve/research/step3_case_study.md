## The Story
Simmons Eye Care is a 3-location optometry practice in central Arkansas serving Benton, Little Rock, and Hot Springs. With Google Reserve activated across all three locations, they generated 696 "Book online" clicks from Google in March 2026 alone, EXCEEDING their combined website clicks (654). Their Benton location achieves a 73% action rate on their Google Business Profile, meaning nearly three-quarters of patients who find them on Google take an action.

The practice uses a 3rd Party Booking Link configuration, pointing the "Book online" button to their own website contact page rather than the built-in PE Scheduler. This demonstrates the flexibility of PatientEngage's Google Reserve integration, which can work with any booking workflow the practice prefers.
