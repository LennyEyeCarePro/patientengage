# Benefits: Google Reserve

## Primary Problem
Optometry practices invest in Google visibility (reviews, SEO, GBP optimization) but lose patients at the conversion step. Without a "Book online" button, patients must leave Google, navigate the practice website, find the scheduler, and complete a form. Each additional step loses patients. Google Reserve eliminates all intermediate steps, capturing patients at the moment of highest intent.

## Top Benefits

### Benefit 1: Capture Patients at the Moment of Intent
When a patient searches "eye doctor near me," their intent to book is at its peak. Google Reserve lets them act on that intent immediately, right from Google Search or Maps, with a single tap. No website navigation. No phone call required. One button to booking.

## Objection Responses

### "Won't Google own my patient data?"
No. Google Reserve is a button that directs patients to YOUR booking page. Google does not receive or store the patient's appointment details, health information, or contact data. The patient clicks through to your system, and from that point forward, everything happens on your terms. This is fundamentally different from Zocdoc, where the patient books within Zocdoc's system and is treated as a Zocdoc customer.

### "Could a third party activate this on my listing without my consent?"
Not through PatientEngage. Google Reserve is a toggle that only you control within your PatientEngage dashboard. No third party can activate it on your behalf. This addresses a real concern in the industry: some practices have reported that platforms like Zocdoc have activated booking on their Google listings without explicit consent. With PatientEngage, nothing happens until you flip the switch.

### "Will this increase my no-show rate?"
Google Reserve links patients to the same booking flow they would use on your website. They still complete your full intake process, select a time, and confirm. The only difference is how they got there: one click instead of three or four. Patients who actively search for and book an appointment have high intent, making impulsive no-shows unlikely.

### "I already have online booking on my website."
Google Reserve is an additional booking channel, not a replacement. It catches patients BEFORE they even visit your website. Data shows that for some practices, Google Reserve clicks match or exceed website booking clicks. It's additive to your existing system.

### "How hard is the setup?"
One toggle. That's it. Navigate to Google Reserve in your PatientEngage dashboard, flip it to On, and choose where the button links. It syncs with Google automatically and goes live within 24 hours. No code. No IT department. No technical work.
