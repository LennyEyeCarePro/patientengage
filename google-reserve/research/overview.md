# Overview: Google Reserve

## What It Does
Google Reserve through PatientEngage adds a "Book online" button directly to your Google Business Profile. When patients search for your practice on Google Search or Google Maps, they see the button and can start booking in one click, without navigating away from Google.

PatientEngage integrates with Google's Reserve with Google program, syncing your practice listing daily. Within 24 hours of activation, the "Book online" button appears on your Google Business Profile across both Search and Maps.

## How It Works

### For the Practice
1. Navigate to Google Reserve settings in your PatientEngage dashboard
2. Toggle Google Reserve to "On"
3. Select your booking type: PatientEngage Scheduler, your own website, or any third-party booking page
4. The system syncs with Google daily
5. Within 24 hours, the "Book online" button goes live on your Google Business Profile
6. Track clicks through the built-in Google Reserve Clicks chart in your dashboard

### For the Patient
1. Patient searches for your practice (or "eye doctor near me") on Google
2. Your Google Business Profile appears in Search results or on Maps
3. Patient sees a prominent "Book online" button
4. On Google Maps, this appears as a full-width light blue banner below the action icons
5. On Google Search, it appears as an action button in the knowledge panel
6. Patient clicks the button and is directed to your chosen booking page
7. Patient completes the booking on your terms, through your system

## The Workflow Narrative
A patient in Benton, Arkansas searches "eye doctor near me" on their phone during lunch. The local optometry practice's Google Business Profile appears with a 4.9-star rating and 790 reviews. Right below the Directions and Save buttons, a blue "Book online" banner catches their eye. They tap it. Instead of navigating a website, they land directly on the booking page. They select an appointment slot for next Thursday, enter their information, and confirm. Total time: under two minutes. The front desk never took a call. The practice website never loaded. But a new patient just booked.

Meanwhile, the practice owner opens their PatientEngage dashboard and sees booking clicks flowing in from Google. No new software to learn. No separate queue. The appointments appear right alongside their existing scheduler bookings.

## Problems It Solves

### Problem 1: Intent Leakage
Patients search on Google with immediate booking intent. Without Google Reserve, they must click "Website," navigate multiple pages, find the scheduler, and complete a form. Each step loses patients. Google Reserve captures them at the exact moment of highest intent.

### Problem 2: After-Hours Booking Gap
When the office is closed, the only option is to call and leave a voicemail (which many patients won't do). Google Reserve provides 24/7 booking access directly from Google, catching the patients who search after 5 PM.

### Problem 3: Competitive Disadvantage
If a competitor practice has "Book online" on their Google listing and you don't, patients will choose the easier path. Google Reserve levels the playing field.

### Problem 4: Wasted Google Investment
Practices invest heavily in Google reviews, local SEO, and GBP optimization. Without Google Reserve, that investment generates visibility but not conversions. Google Reserve turns visibility into appointments.

## Feature Highlights

### Flexible Destination
Unlike competitor platforms that lock you into their own booking system, PatientEngage lets you point the "Book online" button to any URL: the PatientEngage Scheduler, your own website's contact page, a third-party scheduler, or any booking page you choose. Your practice, your rules.

### One-Toggle Setup
No technical implementation required. No code to add. No API configuration. Toggle it on, and it syncs with Google automatically. Live within 24 hours.

### Daily Sync
PatientEngage syncs with Google once per day, keeping your booking status current. The system handles all the technical communication with Google's Reserve platform.

### Click Tracking
Built-in Google Reserve Clicks chart shows your daily click volume over time, so you can see exactly how many patients are using the Book online button and track trends.

### Works Across Search and Maps
The "Book online" button appears on BOTH Google Search and Google Maps, covering the two primary ways patients discover local practices.
