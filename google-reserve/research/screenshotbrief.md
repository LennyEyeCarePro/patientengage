# Screenshot Brief: Google Reserve

## Priority Order and Setup Instructions

### Shot 1 (HERO): ss_gr_01_google_maps_booking.png
**What:** Google Maps listing showing the "Book online" button
**Priority:** HIGHEST : this is the hero image
**Setup:**
1. Open Google Maps
2. Search for a PatientEngage practice with Google Reserve active
3. Click into their listing
4. Scroll to show: Practice name, star rating, review count, action icons (Directions, Save, etc.), and the prominent light blue "Book online" banner
5. Capture the Maps panel with the button clearly visible
**Crop:** Left panel showing the listing from name through the "Book online" button and address below it
**Frame:** Browser chrome frame (red/yellow/green dots) per Style B
**Notes:** The "Book online" button should be the visual focal point. Consider adding a subtle highlight or annotation arrow pointing to it.
