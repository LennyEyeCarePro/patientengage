## PRODUCT FACTS

### What PatientEngage Landing Pages Do:

**Layer 1: Campaign-Targeted Content**
- Each landing page has campaign-specific content at the top (announcement section)
- Content matches the marketing initiative: UV Awareness gets UV content, Dry Eye campaign gets Dry Eye content, Back to School gets pediatric content
- Mirrors the ad's promise -- patient confirms they're in the right place immediately
- Promotional images match the campaign theme

**Layer 2: Practice Data (Auto-Synced)**
The landing page pulls from these PatientEngage sections:
- **Announcements** -- campaign-specific promotional content and images
- **About Us** -- practice description, services list, practice images
- **Locations** -- address, neighborhood description, amenity badges
- **Hours** -- full weekly schedule with split-hour support + special date overrides + real-time open/closed status
- **Our Team** -- doctor headshots, bios, credentials, languages, attributes (Accepting New Patients, etc.)
- **Insurance** -- 40+ insurance plan logos with names
- **Frames** -- frame brand gallery
- **Contact Lenses** -- contact lens brand gallery
- **Testimonials** -- patient reviews with star ratings and reviewer names
- **Google** -- Google Map embed, accessibility attributes

**Layer 3: Scheduling Integration**
Three options depending on the practice's setup:
1. **PatientEngage Scheduler** -- embedded directly with doctor dropdown, exam type dropdown, full calendar grid with available time slots
2. **Contact form** -- for practices without online scheduling
3. **Third-party scheduler button** -- links to the practice's existing scheduling system

### Page Structure (Observed):
1. Header: Practice name, phone CTA, real-time open/closed status, Book button
2. Campaign content: Targeted announcement matching the marketing initiative
3. Scheduler: Embedded booking with doctor/exam type selection and available slots
4. About Us: Practice description, services with checkmarks, photo gallery
5. Location & Hours: Google Map, address, hours, amenity badges (Convenient Hours / Access / On Site Services)
6. Our Team: Doctor photos, bios, credentials, languages, attributes
7. Insurance: Logo grid with 40+ plans, "Show All" button
8. Patient Reviews: Star-rated testimonial carousel
9. Footer: Address, "Powered by PatientEngage," persistent Book button

### How It Works:
1. Practice data already exists in PatientEngage (entered for website, Google, Facebook, directories)
2. Campaign-targeted content is created for the top of the page
3. Landing page assembles from existing data sections -- no copy-paste, no manual content creation
4. Scheduling is configured: PatientEngage Scheduler, form, or third-party link
5. Tracking phone number is assigned
6. Page goes live at the practice's domain
7. When anything changes in PatientEngage, the landing page reflects it automatically

## CASE STUDY FRAME

### The Friction Gap
- Patient clicks ad for "Emergency Red Eye Treatment"
- Lands on homepage with smiling family photo and 15 menu options
- Brain has to work to find what they need
- Most don't. They bounce. The practice paid for that click.

### The Homepage Tax
- Homepage: 2-3% conversion rate
- Landing page: 6-10% average, 15%+ top performers
- Sending paid traffic to homepage = intentionally losing 70% of leads

### The GPS Solution
- Campaign-targeted content mirrors the ad's promise at the top
- One path to one goal: book the appointment
- Practice data auto-synced so the page is always current
- Scheduler embedded directly -- no extra clicks to book

### Composite Quote
> "We were spending $2,000 a month on Google Ads and sending everyone to our homepage. Our conversion rate was terrible. Once we switched to campaign-specific landing pages, the same ad spend started producing real appointments. Nothing changed about the ads. The landing page made the difference."
> -- Practice Owner, independent optometry practice (Composite, anonymized)
