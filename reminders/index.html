Common questions

Things practice owners ask about Recalls & Reminders.

Every question below comes from real conversations with optometry practices. Here are the honest answers.

"My EHR already sends appointment reminders. Why do I need this?" ↓
EHR reminders typically send a single email before an appointment. PatientEngage sends up to 6 touches across SMS, email, and call — starting 7 days before and ending 2 hours before. More importantly, EHR systems do not do recalls: the proactive re-engagement of patients who are overdue for their annual exam. That is where the real revenue recovery happens. Our data shows 431 overdue patients rebooked in a single month across 23 practices. Your EHR cannot do that.
"Won't patients think automated texts are spam?" ↓
Only 1% of patients opt out. Every message is personalized with their name, your practice name, their doctor, and their appointment details. Messages only arrive during business hours (you set the window). And every message includes an easy opt-out. In our network data, 5,209 patients confirmed their appointment via automated messages last month — they are clearly engaged, not annoyed.
"We don't have good email or phone data for all our patients." ↓
Our data shows about 20% of patients have incomplete contact info. But that means 80% of your patients CAN be reached automatically, right now. The system also helps you identify the gap: the "No Contact Info" alert tells you exactly which patients are missing data, so you can capture it at their next visit. Reaching 80% of patients automatically is vastly better than manual calling, where you might reach 30%.
"How is this different from Solutionreach or Weave?" ↓
Three key differences. First, PatientEngage is built exclusively for optometry — we have recall types like Order Pickup and Pre-Appointment Information that generic healthcare platforms do not offer. Second, our multi-touch sequences go deeper: up to 6 touchpoints per recall type with intelligent channel alternation. Third, it is integrated with your PatientEngage platform — Scheduler, Google Reserve, Reviews — one dashboard, one login, one support team. Weave requires buying their entire phone system. Solutionreach is a separate platform with separate training.
"Do patients actually book from a text message?" ↓
Yes. SMS has a 98% open rate versus 20% for email. Messages include a direct booking link that takes the patient straight to your online scheduler. In one month, 431 patients rebooked their overdue exam and 821 rescheduled their appointment — all through automated messages with one-tap booking links.
"How long does it take to set up?" ↓
The recall templates are pre-built with best-practice timing and messaging for optometry. Your CSM activates them during onboarding. You can customize the timing, messaging, and channels for each touchpoint, but most practices use the defaults because they work. Toggle on, and messages start flowing automatically based on your patient data. No complex setup, no IT involvement, no staff training.
"What about Order Pickup notifications? How do those work?" ↓
When a patient's glasses or contacts order arrives, the system sends an automated pickup notification via SMS and email. This is unique to PatientEngage — generic reminder tools do not handle optical orders. 11 practices currently use this feature. It reduces staff time spent calling patients about ready orders and gets patients in faster to pick up their eyewear.
