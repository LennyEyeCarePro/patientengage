# Step 4: Resulting View — Recalls & Reminders

## What the Prospect Should Believe After Reading This Page

### Primary Belief
"PatientEngage Recalls & Reminders will automatically bring back my lapsed patients and confirm upcoming appointments — recovering tens of thousands of dollars in revenue I'm currently losing — without adding any work to my front desk staff."

### Secondary Beliefs
1. **It's already working at scale.** 23 practices, 37,862 messages/month, 5,209 confirmed appointments. This isn't a beta feature — it's proven infrastructure.
2. **Multi-channel is the only way.** 59% of calls go to voicemail. SMS has 98% open rates. The combination of SMS + Email + Call captures patients that any single channel misses.
3. **The ROI is undeniable.** 431 overdue patients rebooked in one month = ~$150,000 in recovered revenue across the network. The system pays for itself many times over.
4. **Setup is simple, not a project.** Pre-built templates, customizable timing, toggle on/off. Not a 6-month implementation.
5. **It respects patients.** Business-hours-only delivery windows, instant opt-out, personalized messages. Patients prefer this to phone calls.

3. **Conviction:** "5,209 appointments confirmed in a month across 23 practices. This works."

### Key Differentiators to Embed
- **6 recall/reminder types** covering the full patient lifecycle (not just appointment reminders)
- **Multi-touch sequences** (not single-message blasts) — Exam Recall has 4 touches over 4 months
- **Multi-channel** (SMS + Email + Call) — not email-only like EHR systems
- **Intelligent timing** — messages sent during business hours, escalating cadence
- **Built for optometry** — triggers based on eye exam dates, eyewear orders, pre-appointment prep specific to vision care
- **Part of PatientEngage platform** — integrated with Scheduler, Google Reserve, and other tools

### What Competitors Can't Match
1. **No Show Recall as a distinct type** — most systems only do forward-looking reminders, not backward-looking recovery
2. **Order Pickup notifications** — unique to eyecare, no generic reminder tool handles this
3. **Pre-Appointment Information** — prep details (insurance card, forms, dilation expectations) sent automatically
4. **EHR integration depth** — triggers based on actual exam dates and appointment data, not manual lists
5. **Network-wide admin view** — for multi-location practices or management groups, one dashboard for all locations
