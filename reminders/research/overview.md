# Overview: Recalls & Reminders

## What It Is
PatientEngage Recalls & Reminders is an automated patient communication system that brings overdue patients back for their annual exams and ensures upcoming appointments are confirmed — all without front desk staff lifting a finger.

## The Problem It Solves
Optometry practices lose 20-30% of patients annually to attrition. Patients forget their annual exam, miss appointments, or simply fall off the radar. Manual phone recalls reach voicemail 60% of the time. The result: empty chairs, lost revenue, and staff buried in unproductive phone work.

## How It Works
The system automates 6 types of patient outreach across the full patient lifecycle:

**Recalls (Re-engaging lapsed patients):**
- **Exam Recall:** Multi-touch sequence starting at 10 months post-exam, escalating through email → SMS → call over 4 months
- **No Show Recall:** Automatic follow-up when a patient misses an appointment
- **Manual Recall:** Staff-triggered outreach for specific patient lists

**Reminders (Confirming upcoming appointments):**
- **Appointment Reminder:** 6-touch countdown from 7 days to 2 hours before, alternating SMS and email
- **Pre-Appointment Information:** Insurance card, forms, and prep details sent before the visit
- **Order Pickup:** Automated notification when glasses or contacts orders arrive

Each recall type uses customizable multi-touch, multi-channel sequences (SMS, Email, Call) with configurable timing and delivery windows.

## Why It Matters
Every lapsed patient represents $300-500 in annual revenue. With 431 patients rebooked in a single month, the system recovers an estimated $150,000 in revenue across the network — roughly $6,650 per practice per month. The system replaces 15-20 hours/month of manual calling and works 24/7.

## Built for Optometry
Unlike generic reminder tools, PatientEngage includes eyecare-specific features: Order Pickup notifications for optical labs, Pre-Appointment prep (dilation info, insurance reminders), and recall timing calibrated to the annual eye exam cycle. Integrated natively with PatientEngage Scheduler and Google Reserve.
