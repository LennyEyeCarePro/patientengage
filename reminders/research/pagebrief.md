# Page Brief: Recalls & Reminders

## Page Purpose
Convert practice owners and office managers who are losing patients to attrition and no-shows by showing them that automated, multi-channel recalls and reminders recover tens of thousands of dollars in revenue — with zero staff effort.

## Target Audience
1. **Primary:** Practice owners/ODs who know they're losing patients but don't have the staff time to do recalls
2. **Secondary:** Office managers/front desk leads who are drowning in manual calling and want their time back
3. **Tertiary:** Multi-location group owners who need visibility into recall performance across all locations
