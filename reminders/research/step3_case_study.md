# Step 3: Case Study — Recalls & Reminders

## Case Study: Multi-Practice Network Performance (23 Practices)

### Overview
Rather than a single-practice case study, the Recalls & Reminders data tells a compelling network-wide story: 23 independent optometry practices using the same automated system to recover lapsed patients and reduce no-shows. The aggregate data is powerful because it shows the system works consistently across different practice sizes, locations, and patient demographics.

### The Challenge
Optometry practices face two related revenue leaks:
1. **Patient attrition:** 20-30% of patients lapse each year, failing to return for their annual comprehensive exam. With average exam revenue of $300-500 per patient visit, a 3,000-patient practice loses $180K-$450K annually to attrition.
2. **No-shows:** The average no-show rate in optometry is 15-20%. Every empty chair costs $150-300 in lost production. A practice with 25 appointments/day losing 4-5 to no-shows loses $600-$1,500 daily.

Before PatientEngage, these practices relied on manual phone calls, paper postcards, or basic EHR email-only reminders. Staff spent 15-20 hours per month on recall calls alone.

### The Solution
PatientEngage Recalls & Reminders automates the entire patient re-engagement and appointment confirmation lifecycle through 6 distinct recall/reminder types:

**Recalls (bringing patients back):**
- Exam Recall: 4-touch sequence from 10-14 months post-exam
- Manual Recall: Staff-triggered recall for specific patients
- No Show Recall: Automatic follow-up when patients miss appointments

**Reminders (keeping upcoming appointments):**
- Appointment Reminder: 6-touch countdown from 7 days to 2 hours before
- Pre-Appointment Information: Prep details sent before visit
- Order Pickup: Notifies patients when eyewear orders are ready

### Revenue Impact (Calculated)
- **431 rebooked patients × $350 avg exam revenue = ~$150,000** in recovered revenue across the network in one month
- **Per practice average: ~19 patients rebooked/month = ~$6,650 monthly recovered revenue**
- **Annualized per practice: ~$80,000 in revenue that would have been lost to attrition**

### No-Show Prevention Impact
- **5,209 confirmed appointments** in one month
- Confirmed patients have 95%+ show rate vs. 80-85% for unconfirmed
- Estimated no-shows prevented: ~520 appointments/month across network
- **At $200 avg per appointment slot: ~$104,000 in protected revenue/month**

### Efficiency Gains
- **37,862 messages sent automatically** — zero staff time per message
- Equivalent manual effort: ~158 hours of phone calls (at 4 min/call, 3 attempts per patient)
- **Staff time saved: ~7 hours/month per practice**
- System operates 24/7 including evenings, weekends, and holidays

### Key Insight: Multi-Channel Matters
The data reveals why multi-channel outreach is critical:
- **59% of calls go to voicemail** — confirming that phone-only recall is broken
- **SMS delivery: instant, 98% open rate** — the primary conversion channel
- **Email: supplementary, good for detailed information** — prep details, booking links
- The combination of SMS + Email outperforms either channel alone
