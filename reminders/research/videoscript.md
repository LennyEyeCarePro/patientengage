# Video Script: Recalls & Reminders

## Video Title
"How PatientEngage Recalls & Reminders Brings Patients Back Automatically"

## Duration Target
90-120 seconds

## Script

### HOOK (0:00 - 0:15)
**[Visual: Empty exam chair, phone ringing with no answer]**

"Right now, 20 to 30 percent of your patients are overdue for their annual eye exam. Your front desk is calling them — and reaching voicemail sixty percent of the time. Every lapsed patient costs you three to five hundred dollars a year. What if they came back automatically?"

### PROBLEM (0:15 - 0:30)
**[Visual: Staff on phone, frustrated, stack of recall postcards]**

"Manual recall doesn't scale. Your team spends fifteen to twenty hours a month calling patients who don't pick up. Postcards cost three dollars each and go straight to the trash. And your EHR? It sends one email reminder. That's it. No texts, no follow-ups, no way to bring back overdue patients."

### SOLUTION INTRO (0:30 - 0:45)
**[Visual: PatientEngage Recalls & Reminders dashboard, toggle switches]**

"PatientEngage Recalls and Reminders automates the entire process. Six recall types cover your full patient lifecycle — from bringing back overdue patients, to confirming tomorrow's appointments, to notifying patients when their glasses are ready."

### HOW IT WORKS (0:45 - 1:10)
**[Visual: Animated sequence showing message flow — email → SMS → call timeline]**

"Here's how Exam Recall works. At ten months after their last exam, the patient gets a friendly email. At twelve months, an SMS with a booking link. If they still haven't booked, the system sends a follow-up email at fourteen months. Each message is personalized with their name, your practice name, and a direct link to schedule."

**[Visual: Appointment Reminder countdown — 7 days, 4 days, 24 hours, 2 hours]**

"For upcoming appointments, the Reminder sequence sends six touches — from seven days before down to two hours before — alternating between text and email. Patients confirm with a single text reply."

### RESULTS (1:10 - 1:30)
**[Visual: Metrics dashboard showing real numbers]**

"In a single month across twenty-three practices: thirty-seven thousand messages sent automatically. Over five thousand appointments confirmed. Four hundred and thirty-one overdue patients rebooked. That's an estimated one hundred and fifty thousand dollars in recovered revenue — with zero staff effort."

### CTA (1:30 - 1:45)
**[Visual: PatientEngage dashboard with Recalls & Reminders toggle]**

"Every month without automated recalls is another batch of patients drifting away. Activate Recalls and Reminders in your PatientEngage dashboard today. Pre-built templates. Multi-channel sequences. Live in hours, not weeks."

**[End screen: PatientEngage logo, "Activate Recalls & Reminders" button, patientengage.ai]**

- Revenue loss angle: $300-500 per lapsed patient per year
- Manual calling is broken: 59% voicemail rate
- Multi-channel approach: SMS (98% open) + Email + Call
- 6 recall types built specifically for optometry
- Network proof: 23 practices, 37,862 messages, $150K recovered
- Eyecare-specific: Order Pickup, Pre-Appointment prep
- Simple setup: Pre-built templates, toggle on
