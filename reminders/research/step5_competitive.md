# Step 5: Competitive Research — Recalls & Reminders

## Competitive Landscape: Patient Recall & Reminder Solutions for Optometry

### 1. Solutionreach
**What they offer:** General healthcare patient communication platform with reminders, recalls, reviews, and marketing automation.
**Pricing:** $300-500/month per location (reported range)
**Strengths:** Large installed base, multi-channel (SMS, email, voice), patient surveys, reputation management integration.
**Weaknesses:**
- Generic healthcare platform, not optometry-specific — no Order Pickup, no Pre-Appointment Information for eyecare
- Complex setup and onboarding (weeks, not hours)
- Separate platform from practice management — another login, another dashboard
- Higher price point for independent practices
- Limited EHR integrations for smaller optometry PM systems

**PatientEngage advantage:** Purpose-built for optometry, integrated with PatientEngage platform (Scheduler, Google Reserve, Reviews), and includes eyecare-specific recall types (Order Pickup, Pre-Appointment Info). Simpler setup, lower cost, unified dashboard.

### 2. Weave
**What they offer:** Phone system + patient communication platform combining VoIP, texting, reminders, and reviews.
**Pricing:** $300-600/month (bundled with phone system)
**Strengths:** Integrated phone system, two-way texting, digital forms, payment collection.
**Weaknesses:**
- Requires buying their phone system — can't just get reminders
- Phone system lock-in is a major barrier to switching
- Recall capabilities are basic compared to multi-touch sequences
- Not purpose-built for optometry
- Expensive for practices that don't need a new phone system

**PatientEngage advantage:** No phone system required. Can activate recalls independently. Multi-touch sequences (4-6 messages per recall type) vs. Weave's simpler reminder approach. Eyecare-specific templates. No hardware lock-in.

### 3. Demandforce (Internet Brands)
**What they offer:** Automated patient communication, appointment reminders, reputation management, and email marketing.
**Pricing:** $200-400/month
**Strengths:** Established brand in dental/optometry, email marketing capabilities, reputation management.
**Weaknesses:**
- Email-heavy approach — SMS capabilities added later and less robust
- Recall sequences are less sophisticated (fewer touchpoints)
- Aging platform with dated UI
- Owned by Internet Brands (same company as WebMD) — data usage concerns
- Limited multi-channel orchestration

**PatientEngage advantage:** Modern multi-channel approach (SMS-first, not email-first). More sophisticated sequencing (6-touch appointment reminders vs. basic email). Real-time metrics dashboard. No data-sharing concerns.

### 4. EHR Built-in Reminders (e.g., OfficeMate, Crystal PM, RevolutionEHR)
**What they offer:** Basic appointment reminders built into the practice management system.
**Pricing:** Included with EHR (no additional cost)
**Strengths:** No extra cost, no extra software, basic appointment reminders.
**Weaknesses:**
- Typically email-only or limited SMS
- Single-touch reminders (one message), not multi-touch sequences
- No recall functionality (can't re-engage overdue patients)
- No no-show follow-up
- No order pickup notifications
- No multi-channel orchestration
- No network-wide reporting for multi-location practices
- Limited customization of timing and content

**PatientEngage advantage:** 6 distinct recall/reminder types vs. basic appointment reminders only. Multi-touch sequences (up to 6 messages). Multi-channel (SMS + Email + Call). Recall capabilities to re-engage lapsed patients. Network-wide admin reporting. 59% of calls go to voicemail — EHR phone-based systems miss most patients.

### 5. RevenueWell
**What they offer:** Patient communication and marketing platform for dental and optometry practices.
**Pricing:** $300-500/month
**Strengths:** Good recall capabilities, patient portal, marketing automation.
**Weaknesses:**
- Primarily dental-focused — optometry features are secondary
- No eyecare-specific recall types
- Separate platform requiring its own dashboard and training
- Higher cost for optometry-specific features

**PatientEngage advantage:** Built exclusively for optometry. Eyecare-specific recall types (Order Pickup, Pre-Appointment Info with dilation/insurance prep). Integrated with the full PatientEngage ecosystem.
