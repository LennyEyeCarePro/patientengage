# Benefits: Recalls & Reminders

## Benefit 1: Recover Lost Revenue from Lapsed Patients
Every patient who skips their annual exam costs your practice $300-500 in lost revenue. The Exam Recall sequence starts reaching out at 10 months and escalates through 4 touchpoints over 4 months — email, SMS, and call — to bring them back before they lapse completely. In March 2026, the system rebooked 431 overdue patients across 23 practices, recovering an estimated $150,000 in revenue that month alone.

**For the practice owner:** This is revenue you're currently losing. Every month without automated recalls is another batch of patients drifting away.

## Benefit 2: Dramatically Reduce No-Shows
The Appointment Reminder sequence sends 6 touches from 7 days before to 2 hours before the appointment, alternating between SMS and email. Patients confirm with a text reply. In one month, 5,209 appointments were confirmed automatically. Confirmed patients show up at 95%+ rates. Each prevented no-show saves $150-300 in lost chair time.

**For the office manager:** No more calling tomorrow's patients every afternoon. The system handles it, and patients actually respond to texts faster than phone calls.

## Benefit 3: Eliminate 15-20 Hours/Month of Manual Calling
Front desk staff currently spend hours calling overdue patients and confirming tomorrow's schedule. 59% of those calls go to voicemail. The system sent 37,862 messages automatically in March 2026 — that's roughly 158 hours of phone calls replaced. Staff can focus on patients in the office instead of patients on the phone.

**For the front desk:** Stop playing phone tag with voicemail. The system does the outreach; you handle the patients who walk in the door.

## Benefit 4: Multi-Channel Reaches Patients Where They Are
Patients prefer texting — SMS has a 98% open rate vs. 20% for email and 41% answer rate for phone calls. PatientEngage uses all three channels in intelligent sequences: email for detailed information, SMS for immediate action, and calls for persistent follow-up. Only 1% of patients opt out, proving patients welcome these communications.

**For patient experience:** No more leaving voicemails that never get returned. Patients get a text, tap a link, and book — in under 30 seconds.

## Benefit 5: Rescue Appointments from Cancellation
When patients can't make their appointment, the system makes rescheduling easy — reply to the text or click the booking link to pick a new time. In one month, 821 patients rescheduled through automated messages. That's 821 appointments that would have been lost without the system. A rescheduled appointment is still a kept appointment.

**For practice revenue:** A canceled appointment is $0. A rescheduled appointment is full production. The system captures that intent.

## Benefit 6: Eyecare-Specific Recall Types No Competitor Offers
Generic reminder tools don't know about optical order pickups, pre-appointment dilation prep, or the annual eye exam recall cycle. PatientEngage includes:
- **Order Pickup** notifications when glasses/contacts arrive (unique to eyecare)
- **Pre-Appointment Information** with insurance card reminders and prep details
- **Exam Recall** timing calibrated to the 12-month eye exam cycle

**For the optometrist:** Your recall system actually understands your workflow — because it was built by people who understand optometry practices.

## Benefit 7: Network-Wide Visibility for Multi-Location Practices
For practices with multiple locations or management groups, the admin dashboard shows recall performance across all locations in one view: messages sent, delivery rates, confirmations, rebooks, and alerts. The March 2026 dashboard showed 23 practices, 15,056 patients, and 37,862 messages — all in a single metrics screen. No per-location logins needed.

**For the multi-practice owner:** One dashboard, all locations. See which locations are performing and which need attention.

## Benefit 8: Setup in Hours, Not Weeks
Recall templates are pre-built with best-practice timing and messaging for optometry. Toggle on, customize if desired, and messages start flowing automatically. No IT involvement, no complex integration projects, no staff training programs. The Appointment Reminder template comes with 6 pre-configured touchpoints that most practices use as-is.

**For the decision-maker:** This isn't a 6-month implementation. It's a toggle switch with immediate impact.
