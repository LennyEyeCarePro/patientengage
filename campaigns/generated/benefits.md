# Benefits — One-Click Campaigns

## Primary Problem
Between appointments, your patients disappear. They see competitor ads. They forget about the specialty services you offer. The only time they hear from you is a recall reminder — and by then, the PE-backed practice down the street has already been in their inbox, their feed, and their search results all month.

You're sitting on thousands of patient records that only hear from you once a year. Not because you don't care, but because nobody on staff has the time, the tools, or the budget to keep your practice visible between visits.

## Top Benefits for the Practice

### 1. Keep patients inside your ecosystem — everywhere they look
Every campaign puts the same message, the same imagery, and the same call to action in front of your patients across email, a dedicated landing page, Facebook, Google, Instagram, and your lobby wall. Between visits, they're not drifting to competitors — they're seeing you. And every touchpoint drives them back to your chair. Coastal Eye Associates sent one allergy campaign and booked 108 appointments — because patients didn't just get an email, they encountered the same campaign on every channel. [OBSERVED — Step 3]

### 2. The landing page keeps visitors inside your world
This isn't email marketing. Every campaign generates a dedicated landing page — branded to your practice, themed to the campaign topic, with a clear appointment CTA. When a patient clicks through from the email or finds you on Google, they land inside your ecosystem, not on a generic website. Coastal Eye Associates saw 10% of campaign recipients visit their landing page — 1 in 10 patients went from email to page to potential booking. [CALCULATED — Step 3]

### 3. Same messaging on social reinforces the campaign without extra work
The Facebook posts, Google Business posts, and Instagram post all carry the same imagery and messaging as the email and landing page. Your patients see the same campaign whether they're checking email at breakfast, scrolling social at lunch, or Googling you before calling. No separate social media strategy needed — it's built into the one click.

### 4. Stop paying an agency for what takes 2 minutes
Marketing agencies charge $1,000–$3,000 a month to manually create campaign materials across channels. They need approvals, feedback cycles, and weeks of lead time. One-Click Campaigns generates 7 branded pieces across 5 channels from a single selection — and schedules them across the month. No invoices. No revisions. No waiting.

### 5. Give your office manager her job back
No more Canva. No more exporting patient lists into a separate tool. No more spending 45 minutes on a social post that gets 2 likes. The campaign handles the creative, the targeting, the scheduling, and the posting. Your office manager gets to manage the practice instead of pretending to be a graphic designer.

### 6. Your specialty services get an automated pipeline
You invested $50,000 in a LipiFlow or an Ortho-K system. Without an automated way to educate patients and drive appointments, that equipment waits for word-of-mouth. One-Click Campaigns has themed campaigns for Dry Eye, Myopia Management, and other specialties — each designed to turn a patient record into a specialty appointment, with the landing page doing the conversion work.

### 7. The numbers prove it works
