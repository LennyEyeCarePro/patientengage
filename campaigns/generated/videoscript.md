# Video Script — One-Click Campaigns
## Total runtime: ~80 seconds

### Act 1 — The Problem (15 seconds)
[SCREEN: A patient scrolling their phone — Instagram ad from a competitor practice, a Google result for "eye doctor near me" showing another practice, an unopened email from their current practice buried in a promo tab.]

NARRATOR: "Between visits, your patients forget about you. They see your competitor's ads. They scroll past other practices on social media. And the only time they hear from you? A recall reminder — six months too late."

[SCREEN: Quick cut — a practice's Facebook page, last post dated 2023. A patient database with thousands of rows, untouched.]

---

### Act 2 — The Solution (50 seconds)
[SCREEN: PatientEngage dashboard — the Campaigns page. Campaign library with themed tiles: Allergies, Dry Eye, Myopia Management.]

NARRATOR: "One-Click Campaigns keeps your patients inside your ecosystem — everywhere they look."

[SCREEN: User clicks 'Eye Allergy Season.' The preview carousel appears.]

NARRATOR: "Pick a campaign topic. One click generates everything — a branded email to your patient list, a landing page that drives appointments, four Facebook posts, four Google Business posts, an Instagram post, a poster for your office, and a new cover photo."

[SCREEN: Slow scroll through carousel — pause on the email (practice logo, booking link), then the landing page (professional layout, appointment CTA), then a Facebook post (same imagery, same message).]

NARRATOR: "Same messaging. Same imagery. Same call to action — across every channel your patient touches. The email drives them to the landing page. The landing page drives the appointment. Social reinforces the message all month."

[SCREEN: The scheduling interface. User picks a date. Animation showing posts distributing across a calendar — weekly cadence.]

NARRATOR: "Schedule it once. It runs for the month."

[SCREEN: Campaign results — email opens, landing page views, appointments animating up.]

NARRATOR: "Coastal Eye Associates sent one allergy campaign to thirty-three hundred patients. Ten percent visited the landing page. Over a hundred booked appointments."

---

### Act 3 — The CTA (15 seconds)
[SCREEN: Split — left side shows a patient drifting (competitor ads, empty inbox, forgotten practice). Right side shows the same patient surrounded by the campaign: email in inbox, post in feed, landing page on screen, poster in the office.]

NARRATOR: "Six hundred forty-seven practices are already keeping their patients close. Stop letting them drift. Start showing up where they are."

[SCREEN: PatientEngage logo + "Book a Demo" button + URL]

NARRATOR: "See it in action. Book a demo today."
