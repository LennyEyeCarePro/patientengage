# Overview — One-Click Campaigns

## What It Does
One-Click Campaigns surrounds your patients with your brand — across every channel they touch — so they never drift between visits. Pick a topic from the campaign library, and the system builds a coordinated marketing campaign: a branded email to your patient list, a landing page designed to trigger appointments, Facebook posts, Google Business posts, an Instagram post, a cover photo, and a printable poster for your office. Same messaging. Same imagery. Same call to action. All pointing back to your chair.

Every piece auto-populates with your practice name, phone number, hours, booking link, and star rating. You don't design, write, or schedule anything. The system handles it.

## Key Workflow
1. **Pick a topic.** The campaign library has optometry-specific themes — Dry Eye Awareness, Eye Allergy Season, Myopia Management, Diabetic Eye Exam, and more — ready to go.
2. **Click to schedule.** The system generates all 7 piece types and schedules them across the month. Social posts go out weekly. The email hits your patient list. The landing page goes live.
3. **Patients stay in your ecosystem.** Whether they open the email, scroll Facebook, search Google, or walk past your front window — they see the same campaign, same branding, same reason to book.
4. **Track what comes back.** Email opens, landing page views, and appointments attributed to the campaign — all in one dashboard.

## The Problem It Solves
Between visits, your patients enter a void. They see competitor ads. They forget about the specialty services you offer. The only time they hear from you is a recall reminder — and by then, the PE-backed practice down the street has already been in their inbox, their feed, and their search results all month.

You know you should be marketing. But the doctor isn't a marketer. The office manager is already doing the job of three people. And the marketing agency wants $1,000–$3,000 a month to manually create what this system generates from a single click.

One-Click Campaigns closes the gap between visits. It keeps your patients inside your world — not with a single email blast, but with a coordinated presence across every channel they touch, all driving the same appointment.
