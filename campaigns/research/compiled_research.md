## PRODUCT FACTS (Steps 1 + 4)

### What One-Click Campaigns Produces (per campaign):
1. **Landing Page** — Full standalone page with practice branding, campaign theme, CTA button
2. **Email** — HTML email with practice logo, campaign imagery, booking link, reply-to practice
3. **Campaign Poster** — Print-ready poster (can be displayed in-office)
4. **Facebook Cover** — Sized for FB page cover photo
5. **Facebook Posts (x4)** — Scheduled weekly, each with unique copy + image
6. **Google My Business Posts (x4)** — Scheduled weekly for GMB profile
7. **Instagram Post (x1)** — Single post with campaign imagery

Total: 14 individual pieces across 5 channels from ONE campaign selection.

### How It Works:
1. Select a campaign from the library (themed: Allergies, Dry Eye, Myopia, Diabetes, etc.)
2. Click to schedule — system auto-generates all 7 piece types
3. Pieces auto-populate with practice name, phone, hours, booking link, review count
4. Social posts auto-schedule across the month (weekly cadence)
5. Email sends to practice's patient list from EHR

### Campaign Library Themes Observed:
- Eye Allergy Season
- Dry Eye Awareness
- Myopia Management
- New Year Vision Benefits
- Diabetic Eye Exam
- Back to School Eye Exams
- And more (seasonal/clinical rotation)

### Merge Fields Auto-Populated:
- Practice Name
- Landing Page Link (unique per practice)
- Phone number, hours, address
- Review count / star rating
- Booking/appointment link

## PATIENT EXPERIENCE (Step 4)

The patient journey through a campaign:
1. Receives branded email with seasonal health topic
2. Opens email — sees practice logo, professional imagery, clear CTA
3. Clicks through to landing page — standalone page with campaign theme, booking button
4. Books appointment via embedded scheduler or calls practice number
5. Meanwhile: sees matching Facebook posts in feed, GMB posts when searching, Instagram post

The experience is cohesive — same visual theme across all channels. Patient sees consistent messaging whether they encounter the campaign via email, social media, or Google search.
